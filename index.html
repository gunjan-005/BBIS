Eligibility Criteria

The Bachelor of Business Information Systems (BBIS) program at Kathmandu University (KU) welcomes students from diverse educational backgrounds.


Who Can Apply

  • Students who have completed 10+2 (Intermediate / equivalent) from a board/university recognized by Kathmandu University.
  • Open to all streams: Science, Management, Humanities, Law, etc.

Foreign Systems / Alternate Credentials

  • GCE A-Level: Must have passed at least 3 subjects in A-Level + 1 general paper at AS-Level (as per KU guidelines).
  • IB (International Baccalaureate): Must pass all 6 courses with at least Grade 4 each and approx. 28 aggregate marks (depending on cycle).

Minimum Grade / Aggregate Requirement

  • At least 50% aggregate or CGPA 2.0 on a 4.0 scale AND at least Grade ‘C’ in each subject.
  • Grade requirements in key subjects must be 40% / CGPA 1.6 with ‘D+’ minimum.

Nationality

Open to both Nepali and non-Nepali nationals.

Note: There is no age limit or work experience requirement.

Admission Process

1

Application Form Submission

  • Forms available online/offline via KUSOM or affiliated colleges.
  • Applicants pay a processing fee (e.g., Rs. 1,500).
2

Entrance Exam Eligibility & Registration

Candidates meeting eligibility sit for the KUUMAT exam.

3

Entrance Exam Results → Writing Test + Interview

Qualified applicants go through analytical writing and personal interview (or GD).

4

Merit List & Selection

Final selection is based on KUUMAT + writing + interview + academic record.

5

Document Verification

Selected candidates submit original documents.

6

Enrollment / Admission Confirmation

After document verification and fee payment, admission is finalized.

Entrance Exam Overview (KUUMAT & Evaluation)

What’s Tested

  • Verbal reasoning
  • Quantitative reasoning
  • Data interpretation
  • English language proficiency

Skills Assessed

  • Critical thinking
  • Analytical ability
  • Logical reasoning
  • English comprehension

Required Documents

Academic Documents

  • 10+2 / equivalent marksheet or transcript
  • 10th/SEE marksheet
  • Migration certificate
  • Character certificate
  • Provisional certificate

Identification

  • Citizenship certificate or passport
  • Passport size photo

Exam & Admission Documents

  • Bank voucher / payment proof for application form
  • Entrance Exam admit card
  • Filled admission form

Any additional document requested by KU SOM

Selection Criteria

  • Entrance exam (KUUMAT) performance
  • Analytical writing test
  • Personal interview / group discussion
  • Past academic performance
  • Overall merit score

KU evaluates motivation, problem-solving, communication, and potential for a technology and business program.
